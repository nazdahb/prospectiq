// Frequently Asked

Quick answers.

Who is ProspectIQ built for? +

Any sales professional targeting local businesses — web designers, SEO specialists, cold callers, appointment setters, roofing and landscaping sales, SaaS agencies, and more.

Do I need technical skills to use it? +

None. Type a city and niche, hit search, and you're done. ProspectIQ handles the data fetching, scoring, difficulty analysis, and pitch generation automatically.

What does the lead score mean? +

Each lead is scored 0–100 based on 12+ signals: website status, Google rating, review count, storm activity, and more. Very Hot = dial now. Cold = skip or nurture later.

What is the Difficulty Rating? +

Every lead gets a predicted close difficulty — Hard, Medium, or Easy — with an AI explanation of why. It factors in competition, online presence, reviews, and engagement signals.

What's the pricing model? +

Credits-based: $29 / $79 / $149 tiers. Monthly subscriptions at $39–$99/mo coming soon. White label available at $249/mo for agencies who want to resell.

Does it integrate with my CRM or GHL? +

GHL integration is in active development — push leads directly with a Pushed badge and timestamp. A full built-in CRM with Excel export is already live and ready to use.
